📧 Deliverability Testing

Monitor email deliverability during campaigns. The system will automatically send test emails to your inbox at regular intervals to verify emails are still being delivered (not going to spam).

When enabled, test emails are automatically sent during campaign sending
Your personal email to check inbox placement (e.g., Gmail, Outlook)
Send test every X emails

💡 How It Works

  1. Enter your personal email address (Gmail, Outlook, etc.)
  2. Set how often you want to receive test emails (e.g., every 50 emails)
  3. Enable auto-testing and start a campaign
  4. During sending, you'll receive test emails at the specified interval
  5. Check your inbox - if tests land in spam, investigate immediately!
